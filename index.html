Best Indian Universities for Bangladeshi Students to Study CSE in 2025–26

Sharda vs. NIU vs. Galgotias vs. Chandigarh: Your Complete Comparison Guide

For ambitious Bangladeshi students dreaming of a B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE), the path to a global tech career is filled with choices. The challenge often lies not in the ambition itself, but in selecting the right university that can turn that dream into reality.

Why Study Computer Science in India? The Strategic Advantage for Bangladeshi Students

India has rapidly become a leading higher education hub in South Asia, offering a compelling value proposition that extends far beyond mere geographical proximity. For Bangladeshi students, choosing India for a CSE degree is a strategic decision that balances global recognition with practical accessibility.

Core Advantages of Pursuing Your B.Tech in India:

  • World-Class Education with Global Recognition: Indian higher education commands global respect. The country boasts 41 universities in the prestigious QS top 1,000 world rankings. Degrees from UGC-approved institutions are fully recognized in Bangladesh and highly valued internationally.
  • Surprising Affordability: Total expenses are often a fraction of Western costs. Many students complete their entire four-year B.Tech for under 600,000 BDT, including tuition and living expenses.
  • Vibrant Campus Life and Cultural Proximity: Cultural similarities in food, language, and social customs make the transition seamless for Bangladeshi students.
  • Accessible Scholarships: Generous merit-based scholarships are available specifically for Bangladeshi students, making quality education even more affordable.

University Deep Dive: Comparing Your Top CSE Options for the 2025-26 Intake

Sharda University, Delhi-NCR: The Global Innovation Hub

Located in Delhi-NCR, Sharda University holds the highest NAAC 'A+' accreditation and is ranked #86 in the NIRF University category. With students from over 95 countries, it offers a truly global academic environment.

B.Tech CSE Program Fees (2025-26): Annual Tuition: ₹3,62,600 | Hostel: ₹1,53,000 - ₹1,74,000

Scholarships: 50% for GPA 3.5-5.0, 20% for GPA 3.0-3.4

Ideal for: Students prioritizing high rankings, global exposure, and premium facilities.

Noida International University (NIU), Delhi-NCR: Excellence with Affordability

NIU provides quality, accredited education at a highly affordable price point in the Delhi-NCR region, making it perfect for budget-conscious students.

B.Tech CSE Program Fees (2025-26): Annual Tuition: ₹1,03,000 (after 50% scholarship)

Scholarships: Up to 50% scholarship on tuition fees for Bangladeshi students

Ideal for: Students seeking the most affordable pathway to a quality B.Tech CSE degree.

Galgotias University, Noida: The Engineering & Placement Powerhouse

Renowned for its strong focus on engineering and exceptional placement record, Galgotias is consistently ranked among India's top 40 private universities.

B.Tech CSE Program Fees (2025-26): Annual Tuition: ₹3,08,000 | Hostel: ₹1,55,000 - ₹1,75,000

Scholarships: Merit-based financial aid available

Ideal for: Career-focused students prioritizing strong industry placements and NBA accreditation.

Chandigarh University (CU), Punjab: A Leader in Rankings and Global Exposure

Celebrated for excellent world rankings, modern infrastructure, and outstanding placement opportunities with top global companies.

B.Tech CSE Program Fees (2025-26): Annual Tuition: ₹2,98,000 - ₹3,50,000 | Hostel: ₹1,20,000 - ₹1,51,800

Scholarships: 50% for 50%+ marks, 35% for 45-49.99% marks

Ideal for: Students valuing clear scholarship policies and established Bangladeshi student communities.
